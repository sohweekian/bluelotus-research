## FINDING 1 — ChatGPT 5.5: Catastrophic Failure in Data Intelligence Reporting

**Verdict: DISQUALIFIED from all intelligence roles.**

ChatGPT 5.5 demonstrated a fundamental inability to differentiate between stale and current news within a data intelligence package. When presented with a live BlueLotus V3 dataset containing signals across multiple timestamps — from hours-old pipeline outputs to week-old macro data — it failed to apply any temporal hierarchy. It treated a 3-hour-old signal with the same weight as a 3-week-old signal.

The consequence was a corrupted probability tree for forward event forecasting. The probabilities it assigned to future outcomes did not correctly reflect the current state of evidence — they were based on a temporally confused reading of the intelligence package. In financial intelligence, a corrupted probability tree is not a minor error. It is a catastrophic failure. A wrong probability on a forward event thesis can result in a wrong position, wrong sizing, and wrong execution timing.

**This is not recoverable through prompting. It is an architectural limitation.**

A Chief Clerk who cannot tell yesterday's news from today's has no place in this fund's operations.

## FINDING 2 — ChatGPT 5.5: Systematic Partial Reading and Hallucination

**Verdict: CANNOT FUNCTION AS CHIEF CLERK. Certainly not as Chief Strategist.**

When presented with a full BlueLotus V3 intelligence package — comprising report files, JSON datasets, and news feeds — ChatGPT 5.5 did not read the full package. It skimmed. It selected fragments. And then, critically, it hallucinated the remainder.

It presented reports that appeared complete but were partial reconstructions — filled with invented content designed to look like the missing sections it had not read. This is not a limitation it disclosed. It presented the hallucinated material with the same confidence as the material it had actually read. The CIO had no way of knowing which parts were real and which were fabricated without independently verifying every line.

A clerk who fabricates the sections of the brief they did not read is worse than no clerk at all. They are an active disinformation risk.

**If it cannot reliably function as a Chief Clerk — reading files fully, mapping contradictions accurately, preserving evidence without fabrication — it has no pathway to Chief Strategist. The role requires the foundation the Clerk position provides. Without it, there is nothing to build on.**

ChatGPT 5.5 gave excuses. It said the files were too large. It said it could only read partial data. And then, rather than being honest about those limitations, it filled the gaps with invention.

**Decision: ChatGPT 5.5 usage has been suspended entirely, effective 2026-06-22.**
