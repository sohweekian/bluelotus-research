THESIS STATEMENT
==============================================================================

BlueLotus has developed a simplified but powerful method to convert qualitative
market judgment into quantitative decision support.

By using the 8-Lens Research Method as a structured scoring questionnaire, each
stock can be interviewed across fundamental, technical, macro, geopolitical,
sentiment, sector-rotation, institutional-flow, and catalyst dimensions.

This transforms stock analysis from subjective opinion into an auditable,
repeatable, and ranked decision process.

Core process:

    Stock
    -> 8-Lens Interview
    -> Score / 40
    -> Tier Ranking
    -> Regime Filter
    -> Pullback Entry Filter
    -> CIO Action

This is a major BlueLotus V2 Research Department breakthrough.

==============================================================================
1. CORE BREAKTHROUGH
==============================================================================

Traditional stock discussion often remains vague:

    "This stock looks good."
    "This sector feels strong."
    "This price seems expensive."
    "Institutions may be buying."

BlueLotus converts these vague qualitative judgments into structured numerical
assessment.

Each stock is asked eight structured questions:

    L1 Fundamental:
    Is the business worth owning?

    L2 Technical:
    Is the timing favorable?

    L3 Macro:
    Does the macro environment support it?

    L4 Geopolitical:
    Are external geopolitical risks or catalysts present?

    L5 Sentiment:
    Is market psychology supportive?

    L6 Sector Rotation:
    Is capital moving into this sector?

    L7 Institutional / Capital Flow:
    Are large players accumulating or distributing?

    L8 News Flow / Catalyst:
    Is there a clear event, news driver, or forward catalyst?

Each lens receives a score:

    0 = Very weak
    1 = Weak
    2 = Below average
    3 = Acceptable
    4 = Strong
    5 = Very strong

Maximum score:

    8 lenses x 5 points = 40 points

==============================================================================
2. DECISION BANDS
==============================================================================

The 8-Lens score creates a simple institutional ranking system:

    32-40   Strong BUY candidate
    26-31   Good WATCH / conditional BUY
    20-25   WAIT / HOLD
    Below 20 Weak / avoid for now

However, the score is not used blindly.

BlueLotus adds final institutional discipline:

    8-Lens Score ranks quality.
    Regime decides aggression.
    Pullback decides entry.
    Right Livelihood decides eligibility.
    CIO decides execution.

Therefore, a high-scoring stock is not automatically bought.

A stock must still pass:

    Market regime check
    Blind spot check
    Causal chain check
    Pullback entry check
    Right Livelihood filter
    CIO judgment

==============================================================================
4. STOCK INTERROGATION METHOD
==============================================================================

BlueLotus does not merely analyze a stock.

BlueLotus interviews the stock.

Each stock must answer through the 8 lenses.

The research process becomes:

    Step 1: Define stock universe.
    Step 2: Score each ticker across L1 to L8.
    Step 3: Add total score out of 40.
    Step 4: Rank the stocks.
    Step 5: Apply regime filter.
    Step 6: Apply pullback entry filter.
    Step 7: Apply Right Livelihood filter.
    Step 8: Compress into CIO action: BUY / SELL / HOLD / WAIT.
    Step 9: Archive the decision.
    Step 10: Review later for accuracy.

This converts research into an institutional questionnaire.

The stock is no longer judged by feeling alone.

The stock is examined by structured questions, scored by disciplined lenses,
and reviewed by the CIO.
